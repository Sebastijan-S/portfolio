About me

A builder who sweats the details

I believe the best digital products are those where the seam between design and engineering is invisible. Working with Figma, Webflow, and Framer, I bridge that gap completely handling everything from the first visual concept to the final, production-ready website without any handoff friction.

As an independent developer, I focus heavily on frontend performance, backend functionality, and seamless animations. Today, I collaborate directly with founders, creative studios, and teams who care about high-level craft and flawless execution as much as I do.

When I'm not at the screen, I'm probably out practicing calisthenics, clearing my mind with a long walk, or losing a game of chess online.
